Who We Are

Team Archco Vortex

We are a robotics team from Egypt participating in the FIRST LEGO League (FLL). We are passionate about robotics, innovation, and teamwork. Our mission is to explore challenges, create creative solutions, and share our discoveries while learning and having fun together.

Our name represents the fusion between archaeology and powerful innovation. "Archeo" reflects discovery, research, and exploring history. The Smart Sieve Project

Automating the process of sorting materials based on size and type.

The Smart Sieve is an innovative project designed to use sensors and electronics to detect different particles and separate them efficiently.

  • ⚡ Saves Time: Makes the sieving process extremely fast.
  • 🎯 Improves Accuracy: Reduces human error in sorting processes.
  • 🤖 Combines Tech: Utilizes robotics, electronics, and AI.
  • 🌍 Real-World Impact: Exploring creative solutions for everyday tasks.

Electronic Components

The brain and muscle behind the Smart Sieve.

ESP32

The brain of the system. Controls all motors, receives input signals, processes data, and sends output signals.

HX711

Operates the load cell by amplifying its small analog signals and converting them to digital data for the microcontroller.

DC Motor

Moves the arm that carries the artifact, providing the core motion for the sorting mechanism.

Mosfet Switch

Works as an electronic switch. Receives a signal from the ESP32 to safely control the vibration motor.

Vibration Motor

Moves the sieve to agitate materials and separate them effectively by size.

🔋

Power & Drivers

Motor drivers to provide high current and protect the ESP32. Powered by Lithium batteries and converters.
